## Get Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Visit `https://app.linksoftware.com` and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Projects">
    Click **Projects** in the left menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Project">
    Click **New Project**. Fill in the name and hit **Create**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Docs">
    Inside your new project, click **Add Document** to start writing!
  </Step>
</Steps>
