---
title: Cannot Log In
description: Quick fixes for login issues in Link Software.
---

## Symptoms

- Login form reloads with no error
- "Invalid credentials" message

## Solutions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check email & password">
    Make sure your email and password are entered correctly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset password">
    Click **"Forgot password?"** on the login page to reset.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Sometimes cached data causes issues. Try clearing your cache and logging in again.
  </Step>
</Steps>
